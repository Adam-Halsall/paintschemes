Adeptus Mechanicus

This entire paint scheme is taken directly from the two Adeptus Mechanicus painting guides for Skitarii and Cult Mechanicus. This was my first army so I wanted to follow a guide.

Kastelan Robots

Kastelan Robots are painted schemes based on the fluff page for them in the codex, it lists a bunch of schemes related to long lost robots. Pick one, good luck.

Death Guard

The Death Guard paint scheme is based on a mix of a couple of inspirations from the codex and other images.

The vectorum inspiration is a cross between The Pallid Hand and Apostles of Contagion.

The goal is to create dirty cream armour with heavy use of bronze metallics, particularly on the shoulder guards. The small armour plates should be trimmed with a brighter green.
