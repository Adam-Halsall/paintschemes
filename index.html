Adeptus Mechanicus

This entire paint scheme is taken directly from the two Adeptus Mechanicus painting guides for Skitarii and Cult Mechanicus. This was my first army so I wanted to follow a guide.

Kastelan Robots

Kastelan Robots are painted schemes based on the fluff page for them in the codex, it lists a bunch of schemes related to long lost robots. Pick one, good luck.

Death Guard

The Death Guard paint scheme is based on a mix of a couple of inspirations from the codex and other images.

The vectorum inspiration is a cross between The Pallid Hand and Apostles of Contagion.

The goal is to create dirty cream armour with heavy use of bronze metallics, particularly on the shoulder guards. The small armour plates should be trimmed with a brighter green.

Base

  2. Drybrush the bash lightly with Averland Sunset

  4. Paint the rim black

Plague Marines

Undercoat

  1. Skull white spray

Cream Armour

  1. Screaming Skull on all armour
  2. Wash heavily with Athonian Camoshade
  3. Clean up armour plate surfaces with Screaming Skull, leaving the recesses and holes/pits gross and green
  4. The rims of armour plates are painted Elysian Green
  5. Add some drips and spills of Athonian Camoshade to holes and cracks in the armour

Flesh Pipes

  1. Paint the metal pieces of pipe inside the flesh Leadbelcher
  2. Clean up any mistakes on the flesh pipes with white
  3. Wash flesh pipes with Reikland Fleshshade, add some Athonian Camoshade in patches if wanting more gross
  4. This is a guideline, there is no strict rule for flesh pipes other than being gross

Horns

  1. Rakarth Flesh
  2. Seraphim Sepia wash
  3. Leaving the base of the horn dark with wash, paint Ushabti Bone over the rest of the horn
  4. Drybrush Mournfang Brown down a third of the way from the top
  5. Drybrush Abaddon Black halfway down the Mournfang Brown from the top

Metal

  1. Leadbelcher
  2. Nuln Oil wash
  3. Potentially use patches of Typhus Corrosion technical paint on things like chainmail

Bronze

  1. Balthasar Gold
  2. Agrax Earthshade wash
  3. Highlight raised areas with Balthasar Gold

Fleshy Poxwalkers

Undercoat

  1. Skull white spray

Everything Else

  1. Two wash layers of Reikland Fleshshade will achieve a caucasian fleshy shade
    • Two wash layers of anything will achieve a decent flesh i.e. a red wash then Reikland Fleshshade will make someone look boiled.
  2. Paint boils a gross green or yellow
  3. Tentacles washed Druchii Violet
  4. All other detail is adhoc/whatever

Green Poxwalkers

  1. Wraithbone spray
  2. One layer of Plaguebearer flesh for bright green
  3. Black Templar contrast paint on horns
  4. Paint all boils wraithbone again
